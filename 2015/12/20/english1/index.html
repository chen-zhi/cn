避免粗俗的英语单词

详见英文版博文：Nasty and Nice Words in English

标题中大小写规则

规则

  1. Capitalize the first and the last word.
  2. Capitalize nouns, pronouns, adjectives, verbs, adverbs, and subordinate conjunctions.
  3. Lowercase articles (a, an, the), coordinating conjunctions(and, but, or, for, nor, etc), and prepositions(on, at, to, from, by, etc).
  4. Lowercase the “to” in an infinitive (I want to play guitar).

表示不开心的词汇

以下摘自念念英文

unhappy

She is very unhappy about having to work on weekends.

upset

I was really upset last week because I didn’t know what to do with the show.

miserable

I feel miserable staying at the office all day.

feeling down

I’ve been feeling down since you left.

bummed / bummed out（不是很礼貌）

I was pretty bummed when my girlfriend dumped me.

好句借鉴与欣赏

  1. Today I don’t feel like doing anything. I just wanna lay in my bed. [取自于歌曲The Lazy Song]
  2. That is for sure / So true
  3. Absolutely horrible
  4. The Europeans were nasty back then
  5. I am greedy to know more and more
  6. At your service / command

    单词列表

    下列单词的意思以及例句全部摘自于Oxford Advanced Learner’s Dictionary(Mac版，收费)

    symbiosis

  7. [U, C] the relationship between two different living creatures that live close together and depend on each other in particular ways, each getting particular benefits from the other
    例：The birds live in symbiosis with the cattle, picking insects from their skin to eat.

  8. [U, C] a relationship between people, companies, etc. that is to the advantage of both
    例：She imagined us living in a perfect mother and daughter symbiosis.

hypnotize

名词为hypnosis

  1. [Verb]~ sb to produce a state of hypnosis in sb
  2. [Verb][usually passive] ~ sb (formal) to interest sb so much that they can think of nothing else
    例1: He was hypnotized by her beauty.
    例2：She gazed down into the ocean, hypnotized by the swirling tide.

villain

  1. the main bad character in a story, play, etc.
    例：He often plays the part of the villain.

  2. a person who is morally bad or responsible for causing trouble or harm
    例：the heroes and villains of the 20th century
